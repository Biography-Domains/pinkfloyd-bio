CHRONOLOGICAL HISTORY

Timeline

None

Formation of the band

Syd Barrett, Roger Waters, Nick Mason, and Richard Wright form Pink Floyd in London.

August 5

Release of 'The Piper at the Gates of Dawn'

Pink Floyd's debut album is released, a seminal work of psychedelic rock.

None

Syd Barrett's departure

Syd Barrett leaves Pink Floyd due to mental health issues and is replaced by David Gilmour.

March 1

Release of 'The Dark Side of the Moon'

Pink Floyd's breakthrough album is released, becoming one of the best-selling albums of all time.

November 30

Release of 'The Wall'

Pink Floyd's rock opera is released, exploring themes of isolation and paranoia.

None

Roger Waters leaves Pink Floyd

Roger Waters announces his departure from Pink Floyd, leading to a legal battle over the band's name.

None

Release of 'A Momentary Lapse of Reason'

Pink Floyd, led by David Gilmour and Nick Mason, releases their first album without Roger Waters.

None

Release of 'The Division Bell'

Pink Floyd releases their final studio album with David Gilmour, Nick Mason, and Richard Wright.

July 2

Pink Floyd reunion at Live 8

Pink Floyd reunites with Roger Waters for a performance at the Live 8 concert in London.

July 7

Syd Barrett's Death

Syd Barrett dies at his home in Cambridge at age 60.

September 15

Richard Wright's Death

Richard Wright dies in London after a short battle with cancer at age 65.

None

Release of 'The Endless River'

Pink Floyd releases their final album, based on unreleased material from 'The Division Bell' sessions.
