Pink Floyd

Progressive Rock, Psychedelic Rock, Art Rock, Experimental Rock

Pink Floyd, formed in London in 1965, stands as one of the most influential and commercially successful progressive rock bands of all time. Their experimental sonic landscapes, philosophical lyrics, elaborate live shows, and iconic album art have captivated generations. The band's origins trace back to the coming together of Syd Barrett, Roger Waters, Nick Mason, and Richard Wright.
